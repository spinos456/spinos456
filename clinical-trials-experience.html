Clinical Trials Research Experience

Laboratory professionals, including a team of certified pathologists, Quality Manager , and Experienced staffs and dedicated Project Managers, who take full ownership of all aspects of your study, we can efficiently manage and provide expert analysis for your clinical trial research programs.

Clinical research objectives include:

☆  Testing methods for detecting a disease early, sometimes before symptoms appear,

☆  Finding ways to prevent a health problem, including in people who are otherwise healthy but are at a higher risk of developing a disease

☆  Improving the quality of life for people suffering from a life-threatening disease or a chronic health condition

☆  Investigating carer's or support group's roles

SpinoS was established with an aim to build a full-fledge CRO offering end to end service with respect the phase studies, with system and process in compliance with global regulatory standards.

Unsurpassed Project and Data Management

Our Project Management team has extensive experience conducting clinical trials and is available around the clock to offer in-depth counsel throughout the entire project lifecycle. Committed to delivering unsurpassed service, we assign a dedicated Project Manager who will act as your central point of contact throughout the entire project and become well-versed in your requirements and processes. This helps us adapt our central lab services to the unique needs of your study, by providing customized materials and procedures, speciality tests, and convenient sample collection kits that can be branded to support product commercialization.

Data Management

☆  Our Data Management team stays involved from the onset to ensure seamless integration throughout the entire study lifecycle. We provide laboratory results in a wide variety of formats, offer a full range of data transfer options, we can deliver customized reports designed to meet the specific requirements of your clinical trials research.

☆  Under the overall control of a Global Project Manager, every new study is in the hands of an experienced professional who guides our clients through the intricacies of running a global trial.
