SpinosLifescience and Research Pvt. Ltd


SpinoS Life Science and Research Private Limited is an independent full-fledged Clinical Research Organization based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India pursuing Bioequivalence studies and Clinical trials. SpinoS has seven years of impeccable regulatory history with DCGI, USFDA, WHO, NPRA, and NABL. SpinoS holds codes of conduct and best practices for research integrity. Co-ordinated principles and strategies for the Pharma/Biotech/Cosmetic/Derma/Medical Device/ industry. Within a short-span of time SpinoS had completed more than 300 BA/BE studies. We support Clinical research in multiple dimensions in different elements of scientific investigation. Simply put, it involves human participants and helps translate basic research (done in labs) into new treatments and information to benefit patients.
